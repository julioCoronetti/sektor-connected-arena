## Slide 4 — Gamification & AI

**Title:** Why fans keep coming back

**Layout:** 2 columns

**Left — Engagement loop:**

```
Match event (goal, foul)
        ↓
Bedrock generates personalized prediction
        ↓
Fan answers within 15s
        ↓
Correct → Points + Streak + Badge
        ↓
Leaderboard updates live
        ↓
PressureBar rises
        ↓
Next event...
```

**Right — Mechanics:**
- 🎯 Points per correct answer (10 base, 20 with GPS)
- 🔥 Streaks: consecutive correct answers
- 🏅 7 unlockable badges
- 📊 Live leaderboard per match
- 💬 Forum sentiment via Bedrock
- 📍 2x multiplier for in-stadium fans

## Design tips

- Background: `#0F0F0F` (Sektor black)
- Titles: white `#F5F5F5`, bold, 28-32pt
- Accents: red `#CC0000`
- Body text: gray `#888888`, 14-16pt
- Screenshots: rounded corners, subtle shadow
- Use no more than 3 colors per slide
- Export as PDF before zipping
